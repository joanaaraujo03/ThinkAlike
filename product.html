Introducing Generator

Generator is our AI-powered tool that helps educators and institutions create personalized educational content quickly and efficiently. From multiple choice questions to realistic scenarios and detailed summaries, Generator delivers what you need — fast.

Scalable

Generate thousands of questions in seconds, tailored to your needs.

Personalized

Customize topic, difficulty, format, and more — before or after generation.

Organized

Use folders to keep your content structured and easily accessible.

Exportable

Download content in PDF, Excel, JSON, CSV, or Moodle XML formats.
